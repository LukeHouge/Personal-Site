Luke

Luke has been interested in technology his entire life, learning as much as he can every turn of the way. Cisco CCNET and CompTIA A+ certified, he is a professional in networking and computing. Luke built his first computer when he was 10, and has been experimenting with networking gear and computers ever since. At home he manages an enterprise style network, and has experience installing various other networks. Website design is a passion, see his personal site at lukehouge.com. He has placed 2nd in the nation two years in a row for Business Professionals of America's Website Design competition (for his sites techlybuilds.com and bpacares.com. He has also developed and deployed websites for multiple clients. Home theater and audio are also passions, having designed, troubleshooted, and consulted for multiple installs. Luke is an experience first, attention to detail, and result driven technology expert, determined to meet your needs.
