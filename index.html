About

Hi, my name is Luke. I am a Freshman studying Computer Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin- Madison and enjoy coding. I love computers and everything to do with them. I have experience with website design, development, and deployment using HTML5 and CSS, and both front and back-end projects in Java. I do a lot of full-stack web development and system administration both for my work, and for fun. At home I maintain a server rack with various Dell Servers running numerous VM’s to experiment on. They are running Ubuntu, with VMware ESXi acting as the hypervisor, and veeam providing backups. I am also a part of the University of Wisconsin’s Hyperloop team- Badgerloop where I am active on the software and website teams.
